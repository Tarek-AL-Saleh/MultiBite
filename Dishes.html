Dish Name

This dish features succulent shrimp, perfectly seared and tossed in a rich garlic butter sauce with a hint of spice. The shrimp are cooked until golden and tender, with the garlic butter sauce infusing them with a deep, savory flavor. A touch of red pepper flakes adds just the right amount of heat, balancing the richness of the butter. The dish is served over a bed of fragrant lemon rice, which adds a zesty, refreshing contrast to the bold flavors of the shrimp. Garnished with fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon, this dish is a delightful blend of savory, spicy, and citrusy flavors.
